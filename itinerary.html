Il percorso

Giorno 1

Naturalistic exploration itinerary. Visit to the waterfalls of Castel Giuliano, Caldara di Manziana, Bosco di Betulle Bianche and Bosco Macchia grande.

Giorno 2

Historical-cultural exploration itinerary. Visit of the most important points of interest in Viterbo.

Giorno 3

Historical-cultural exploration itinerary. Visit to the historic center of Montefiascone and the municipality of Bagnoregio.

Giorno 4

Historical-archaeological exploration itinerary. Visit to the Museum of Civita and the Necropolis of Pianezze, Centocamere and the Tufo crucifix.
